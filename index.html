Kdb+ on GitHub

The Fusion interfaces for kdb+ are

  • written for non-kdb+ programmers to use
  • well documented, with understandable and useful examples
  • maintained and supported by KX on a best-efforts basis, at no cost to customers
  • free for all use cases, including 64-bit and commercial use
  • released under the Apache 2 license

Designed from the start for extreme scale, and running on industry-standard servers, KX Technology has been proven to solve complex problems faster than any of its competitors.
